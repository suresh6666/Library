Memberships

Member driven Library Management System

LetUsRead library management system is built in such a way that our members can do all the membership functions themselves; opening and closing of memberships, adding addresses, updating checkout parameters, creating/renewing/closing reading plans, and even checking out of materials.

Safe and Secure Membership Accounts

We have built a financial module for LetUsRead that beats that of a banking application. Membership account keeps track of all financial transactions that are taking place in memberships. Members can see the full details of all transactions all the time. Membership account balance is always safe and secure and belongs to its owners ï¿½ the members. Membership account is never debited without its memberï¿½s knowledge.

Managed Members

Members without computers can also become LetUsRead members. All they have to do is to call the library to setup their memberships. Then when a service is needed, they call the library to perform the required membership function for them. This is a very useful feature for senior citizens and others who may not have access to computers.

Apartments/Corporate/Group Memberships

People living in apartments or working together in offices or associated together in other ways as in clubs and shops can have single membership with higher security deposit and longer reading plans to help them check out many materials at a time and share among themselves.

Multiple Addresses

Members are allowed to maintain three addresses. LetUsRead uses Indian postal code based addressing; members can search for their cities and add addresses helping accurate addressing.

Referral Scheme

Spread the joy of reading! Members get referral incentive when the referred members spent a minimum reading fee with LetUsRead. Referral process is fully automated and members always know the statuses of their referrals any time online. There is no limit on the number of referrals one can make.

Incentive Amount

From time to time LetUsRead awards incentive amounts to its members to encourage them read more. Incentive amounts are directly credited to membership accounts.

Sponsorship Feature

LetUsRead also features a sponsorship account towards which members can contribute amount. These funds are awarded to less-privileged school children for their reading needs whose eligibility is determined by working with respective schools.

Member Book Requests

If a book is not available with our master-catalog, members can use the feature ï¿½Request a Bookï¿½ to let LetUsRead know their interest in the book.

Wish List Redefined

LetUsRead employs a multi-purpose wish list to help our members. When members add publications to their wish list, they are automatically grouped into one of the four sections; out-of-stock books, out-of-stock magazines, in-stock books and in-stock magazines.

Members can add publications to their wish list even if LetUsRead have no stock of those publications; doing so will help us know what our members like to read and we use this information to stock materials as required.

If members opt for, publications from out-of-stock book list can be automatically checked out when they become available.

Down Payment

If LetUsRead donï¿½t have copies of a book that our members want to read, members can make a down payment for it to express their interest to read the book. In that case, LetUsRead make every effort to procure copies of it as soon as possible.

Down payments are debited from membership accounts and they are automatically credited back to membership accounts once members borrow, read and return the books for which they have made the down payment.

Share Your Books

Do you have a collection of books that you bought over the years gathering dust on your books shelves? Why not think of sharing those books with us and let other members also read them?

When you share books with us, you earn a share of its reading fee when your materials are borrowed.

Members can keep track of all their shared materials, its transactions and circulation history from their membership account online.

Member Recommended Magazine Subscriptions

Members can express their interest on any magazines from our master magazine list. Based on the demand, we may decide to subscribe to those magazines.

Membership Email Notifications

All important membership messages are sent to members through emails. Members get plan notification messages, if their plan is not set to renew or if their account does not have sufficient balance to renew their plans.

Membership SMS Notifications

Members can sign up to receive important membership messages through SMS.

Membership Messages

All important membership messages will also be listed on online. These messages include checking out of books, issuing, returning, late fees, renewals warning messages etc.

Borrowing

Flexible Security Deposit

LetUsRead members can choose their security deposit.

When members want to check out more books at a time they can increase their deposit. The security deposit determines the number of publications that members can keep at a time.

Flexible, Affordable and Sensible Reading Plans

Pay-on-time and pay-in-advance plans. In pay-on-time plan (the default plan), members can keep the checked out publications for two weeks. In Pay-in-advance plans, members can keep their publications for one month upto 12 months.

Early Return Credit

Though members can keep the issues publications as long as they have an active plan, they are encouraged to return them as soon as they have read them. If publications are returned within a specified number of days (depends on the material group of the publication), as an incentive, a percentage of the reading fee of the publication is credited back to their active plan.

Please check the page ï¿½Reading Schemesï¿½ on our home page to know various material groups and its early-return-days and early-return-credit-rates.

Borrow Again Incentive

For some publications, members are awarded borrow again incentive if they borrow the same publications again. Incentive is calculated as a percentage of the reading fee of the publication.

Reading Discount

Some publications are given further discount on the already low reading fees. Members can see the discount on the book details page.

Circulation

Advanced Reservation System

LetUsRead has an advanced and dynamic reservation system to process member requests for publications. Members can reserve the publications for checkout if they are not available for immediate checkout.

Delivery/Collection Services

LetUsRead offers delivery/collection services to/from member addresses. A list of pin codes where we serve is listed on our library home page.

Multiple Issue/Return Methods

Members can choose a default issue-method for their memberships. Issue-method can be delivery or pick-up. They can also set issue-method of each individual item on their checkout queue that could be different from their default issue-method. By default return-method is set according to the issue-method. If the issue-method is delivery, return-method is set as collection otherwise return-method is set as drop-off. However, members can set return-method of individual issued materials to their choice.

Get New Materials Issued While Holding Old Ones

You can get the new materials issued to you while holding the old ones. All you have to do is to set the ï¿½Publication ready for returnï¿½ flag on the circulation details of the checked out material. This helps the system to check out more materials than what is allowed by their security deposit on the assumption that when the newly checked out materials are issued to you, you return the old ones.

You Will Never Read the Same Book Twice!

While you are always welcome to read a book any number of times, system will warn you when you are about to add a book to your checkout queue if you have already read it.

Automatic Checkout

Members can opt for automatic checkout feature and add their favourite books to their checkout queue and set the frequecy of checkout and limit of reading fee. Books will automatically checked out and issued to members according to the automatic checkout parameters. This is a good feature for children to make books regualry available to them to encourage reading.

Financials

Account Driven Financial Model

In LetUsRead, members must first credit their membership account with money before that money can be used for any use within their memberships. Members can maintain a balance in their account and use it for charges and fees when they incur.

Payment Gateway

Members can use their credit/debit cards to credit their membership account. Money is immediately credited and members can see their account balance online.

Account Transfer

Members can transfer amount from their membership accounts to any other membership accounts. This is a useful feature for parents who are supporting their children in their membership fees. Let children activates their login accounts and then parents transfer money from their membership accounts to their childrenï¿½s membership accounts. Children will never have to deal with membership financials.

Catalog

Catalog

We keep adding books to our catalog regularly. This will help members to express their wish to read out-of-stock books.

Master Magazine List

Our master magazine list contains all popular magazine information and is updated regularly. Members can express interest on any of these magazine and we will try to subscribe to those magazines based on the demand for it.

Popular Magazine Subscriptions

We have subscribed to a number of popular magazines. Members can express their interest in any non-subscribed magazines from the master magazine list. Based on the number of requests receive, we may decide to subscribe to those magazines.

Material Groups

To give best lending services to our readers, publications are grouped into various material groups with each group having its own lease-rates, early-due-days and early-return-credits-rates.
