Questions?

How do I pay my membership dues?

You can pay your membership dues at the time your checked out materials are delivered to you. You can also pay your dues using credit/debit cards from your membership page using the link 'Credit My Account'.

Once I'm done reading the materials, how do I return them?

You can either drop-off the material at the library or say that you are done with reading at your 'My Checked Out Materials' page in which case we will come and collect your materials from your address.

Can I call the library for any service I need?

Of course! You never have to use our web site if you don't want to but still can read our books! Call the library for any service from setting up the membership to checking out publications and returning them.
